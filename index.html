About

Beautiful Daniela Andrade was born on the 15th day of August 1992 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. Although she is Canadian by birth, her parents are from Honduras, so she is of Honduran ethnicity.

Coming from a family that sings, singing is something that is almost natural to her. According to Daniela. she grew up surrounded by singers, apart from her mom who wasn’t that of a great singer, her three older siblings sing wonderfully well and her dad was a choir director and he taught her to sing harmonies from a very tender age.

She published her first video in October and it was the cover of “Say It’s Possible” by Terra Naomi, which was followed by her other achingly lonely and acoustic renditions of songs which included Drake’s Hotline Bling, Radiohead’s Creep, Gnarls Barkley’s Crazy and many others. These increased her video viewership which is over 254 million, as well as her subscribers base that is above 1.6 million.

Her favorite cover song is Falling by The Civil Wars. Her music influencers include Ella Fitzgerald, Damien Rice, Etta James, Regina Spektor, Jeff Pianki, Sondre Lerche,  Feist, The Staves, Lucy Rose, and many others.
